Welcome to Mad For Books!


We are a book club focusing on developing your childrens reading ability through focused fun filled group reading sessions, 1 2 1 tuition, and fancy dress!


Reading doesn't just unlock your childs imagination, it improves their creativity , enhances vocabulary and develops concentration!


As a group of passionate parents, we decided to create Mad for Books to promote the enjoyment and fulfillment of reading books with your children and the key skills it can give them in preperation for further education.


We welcome children of all reading abilities and our main priority is that your child simply enjoys themselves and starts to see reading as fun exciting activity!

Our Mission!

Open Their Imagination


Our book dress up sessions are fantastic opportunities to bring your childrens favourite books to life. This aids both in their understanding of the story but also allows them to become creative!

Improve Their Vocabulary


Our group reading sessions encourage the children to read aloud and learn from each other! This way the children can gain confidence in their language and have fun at the same time!

Reading is fun!


One of our main priority is to ensure the children look at reading as a fun filled activity they look forward to! This makes reading time at home more enjoyable and engaging!
