Welcome to Mad For Books!


We are a book club focusing on developing your childrens reading ability
through focused fun filled group reading sessions, 1 2 1 tuition, and fancy dress!


Reading doesn't just unlock your childs imagination,
it improves their creativity , enhances vocabulary and develops concentration!

Our Goals

Open Their Imagination


Our book dress up sessions are fantastic opportunities
to bring your childrens favourite books to life.
This aids both in their understanding of the story
but also allows them to become creative!

Improve Their Vocabulary


Our group reading sessions encourage the children to read
aloud and learn from each other! This way the
children can gain confidence in their language and
have fun at the same time!

Reading is fun!


One of our main priority is to ensure the children look
at reading as a fun filled activity they look forward to!
This makes reading time at home more enjoyable
and engaging!
